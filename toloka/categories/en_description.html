English description

In this task, you should choose the best category for news. There are 8 categories: Society, Economy, Sports, Technology, Entertainment, Science, Other, Not news. The description of each category and the examples are below. There is an additional option in the case of the non-English article.

Not news

  • Timeless opinion articles (“Why democracy isn't suitable for the Middle East”).
  • Historical articles (“How the Portuguese first went around Africa”)
  • Encyclopedia-style articles, reference, tips, how-to's (“Kenyan wildlife”, “How to choose a router”)
  • Lists without anything happening (“Six reasons to stay home for Christmas”, “7 wonders of the modern world”, “Best laptops of 2019”).
Learn how to make Excel your b*tch with these online courses — Not news (how-to)
The ultimate list of greatest opening lines in movie history — Not news (list without anything happening)
Why democracy isn't suitable for the Middle East — Not news (timesless opinion article)
How the Portuguese first went around Africa — Not news (histortical article)
How to choose a router — Not news (how-to)

Society

Politics, elections, legislation, incidents, crime, and so on. The biggest category. Includes laws passed and planned, debates in government and other political occurrences, crimes, disasters of any scope, etc.

Two Cannabis-Based Medicines Have Been Approved For Use By The NHS — Society
